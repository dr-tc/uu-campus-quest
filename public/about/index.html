Campus Quest was designed and developed by Dr. Thérèse Charles, a lecturer in Game Design and Development at the Derry~Londonderry campus.

This project is a collaborative effort with the Student Success Centre , aimed at enhancing the 1st Year induction experience during Welcome Week.
